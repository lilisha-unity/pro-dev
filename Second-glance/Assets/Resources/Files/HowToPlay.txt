1. Select <b>Start</b> to begin.
2. The card will change automatically after a few seconds to show a new object.
3. If the object on the card has appeared before, select the card to score points.
4. If it’s a new object, don’t click — just wait for the next card.
5. Gain points for clicking on repeated objects.
6. Lost points for clicking on new objects or missing repeated ones.
7. As the game continues, the card changes more quickly, making it harder to remember past objects!
8. The game ends when you run out of lives.
